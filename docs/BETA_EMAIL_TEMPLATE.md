### Pro Tips for Beta Testing

- **Try the API** (if you're technically inclined) — Enterprise features are unlocked during beta
- **Test edge cases** — Try weird symbols, extreme price ranges, large watchlists
- **Mix asset classes** — Test stocks, crypto, and forex together in one watchlist
- **Go back in time** — Use historical data viewer to backtest your own alerts
- **Break stuff** — Seriously. That's how we find bugs.
