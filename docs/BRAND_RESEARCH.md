### 🎯 Key Findings

1. **X Handle Challenge**: ALL X handles in the candidate list are taken/unavailable. Consider:
   - Registering with alternative handles (e.g., @luminostrading_official, @LuminosTrade)
   - Building audience through LinkedIn/professional channels first
   - X handle might be less critical for B2B fintech platform

2. **Domain Availability**: ~50% have at least one extension available
   - .ai extension: Best availability (many open)
   - .com: Lowest availability (most competitive)
   - .io: Moderate availability (good alternative)

3. **Recommendation Priority**:
   - **First choice**: Luminostrading (unique, all domains free, sophisticated feel)
   - **Second choice**: Tradvue (direct fintech naming, all domains free, punchy)
   - **Third choice**: Citadeltrading (premium, all domains free, strong brand)

4. **Strategy Suggestion**: 
   - Secure all three domain extensions once you pick
   - Use .com as primary
   - Use .io or .ai for secondary brand positioning (e.g., "app.tradvue.com" or "api.tradvue.ai")
   - Build social presence with available handles + branding
