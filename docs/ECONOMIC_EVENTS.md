# US Economic Calendar - Major Events

Comprehensive list of market-moving US economic releases with typical impact and timing.

---

## Employment Data

### Nonfarm Payroll (NFP) Report
- **Release Schedule:** First Friday of each month
- **Release Time:** 8:30 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** CRITICAL (Highest)
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Total nonfarm payroll employment (job creation)
  - Unemployment rate
  - Average hourly earnings (wage growth)
  - Participation rate
  - Prior month revisions
- **Significance:** Directly influences Fed policy decisions and market valuations
- **Typical Volatility:** High (50+ point S&P swings common)

### Weekly Jobless Claims
- **Release Schedule:** Every Thursday
- **Release Time:** 8:30 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior week (week ending Saturday)
- **Market Impact:** HIGH
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Initial jobless claims
  - Continuing claims (4-week average)
  - Insured unemployment rate
- **Significance:** Real-time labor market indicator
- **Typical Volatility:** Moderate (visible but less than NFP)

### Job Openings & Labor Turnover Survey (JOLTS)
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (mid-month)
- **Release Time:** 10:00 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** MEDIUM-HIGH
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Job openings
  - Quit rates
  - Hiring rates
  - Labor turnover rates
- **Significance:** Indicates labor market tightness and wage pressure
- **Typical Volatility:** Moderate

## Inflation Data

### Consumer Price Index (CPI)
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (mid-month)
- **Release Time:** 8:30 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** CRITICAL (Highest)
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Headline CPI (all items)
  - Core CPI (excluding food & energy)
  - Monthly vs. year-over-year changes
  - Shelter/rent component
  - Energy prices
- **Significance:** Primary inflation gauge; Fed policy target
- **Typical Volatility:** Very High (100+ point S&P swings common)

### Producer Price Index (PPI)
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (first business day of month)
- **Release Time:** 8:30 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** HIGH
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Headline PPI (all items)
  - Core PPI (excluding food & energy & trade)
  - By stage of processing
  - Demand/supply pressures
- **Significance:** Early inflation signal; input costs for businesses
- **Typical Volatility:** High

### Personal Consumption Expenditures (PCE) Index
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (end of month)
- **Release Time:** 8:30 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** HIGH
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Headline PCE
  - Core PCE (Fed's preferred inflation gauge)
  - Monthly vs. year-over-year changes
- **Significance:** Fed's preferred inflation measure
- **Typical Volatility:** High

## Growth & Output

### Gross Domestic Product (GDP)
- **Release Schedule:** Quarterly (3 releases per quarter: "advance," "second," "final")
- **Release Times:**
  - Advance Estimate: ~4 weeks after quarter ends, 8:30 AM ET
  - Second Estimate: ~2 months after quarter ends, 8:30 AM ET
  - Final Estimate: ~3 months after quarter ends, 8:30 AM ET
- **Market Impact:** CRITICAL
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Real GDP growth rate (annualized)
  - Personal consumption
  - Business investment
  - Government spending
  - Net exports
  - Price index component
- **Significance:** Overall economic health; recession indicator
- **Typical Volatility:** Very High on advance estimate

### Industrial Production & Capacity Utilization
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (mid-month)
- **Release Time:** 9:15 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** MEDIUM-HIGH
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Industrial production (overall, manufacturing, utilities, mining)
  - Capacity utilization rate
  - Prior month revisions
- **Significance:** Manufacturing health; inflation signal
- **Typical Volatility:** Moderate

### Manufacturing ISM Index (Purchasing Managers Index)
- **Release Schedule:** First business day of each month
- **Release Time:** 10:00 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** HIGH
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Overall PMI (50+ = expansion, <50 = contraction)
  - New orders
  - Production
  - Employment
  - Supplier deliveries
  - Inventories
  - Prices paid
- **Significance:** Early indicator of manufacturing health and inflation
- **Typical Volatility:** Moderate-High

### Services ISM Index
- **Release Schedule:** First business day of each month (delayed from Manufacturing PMI)
- **Release Time:** 10:00 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** HIGH
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Overall PMI
  - Business activity
  - New orders
  - Employment
  - Prices
- **Significance:** Largest sector of US economy; critical for growth assessment
- **Typical Volatility:** Moderate-High

## Consumer Spending & Sentiment

### Retail Sales
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (mid-month)
- **Release Time:** 8:30 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** HIGH
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Total retail sales (including & excluding autos)
  - Core retail sales (excluding gas/autos)
  - Month-over-month & year-over-year changes
  - By sector breakdown
  - Prior month revisions
- **Significance:** Consumer spending is ~70% of GDP; early demand signal
- **Typical Volatility:** High

### Advanced Retail Sales
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (~10 days before full report)
- **Release Time:** 8:30 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month (preliminary)
- **Market Impact:** HIGH
- **Key Metrics:** Preliminary retail sales
- **Significance:** Early indicator before full release
- **Typical Volatility:** High

### Consumer Confidence Index (Conference Board)
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (end of month)
- **Release Time:** 10:00 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Current month (survey-based)
- **Market Impact:** MEDIUM-HIGH
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Present situation index
  - Expectations index
- **Significance:** Consumer sentiment affects spending behavior
- **Typical Volatility:** Moderate

### University of Michigan Consumer Sentiment
- **Release Schedule:** Twice monthly (mid-month preliminary, end-of-month final)
- **Release Time:** 10:00 AM ET (preliminary), 9:55 AM ET (final)
- **Data Covers:** Current month (survey-based)
- **Market Impact:** MEDIUM
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Overall sentiment index
  - Current conditions
  - Future expectations
  - Inflation expectations (important for Fed)
- **Significance:** Real-time consumer mood; inflation expectations
- **Typical Volatility:** Moderate

## Housing Data

### Housing Starts & Building Permits
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (mid-month)
- **Release Time:** 8:30 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** HIGH
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Total housing starts (annualized)
  - Single-family vs. multifamily
  - Building permits
  - Completions
- **Significance:** Leading indicator of economic activity; construction jobs
- **Typical Volatility:** High (volatile month-to-month)

### New Home Sales
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (late month)
- **Release Time:** 10:00 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** MEDIUM-HIGH
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Total new home sales (annualized rate)
  - Median sales price
  - Months of supply
  - Geographic breakdown
- **Significance:** High-ticket item; economic health indicator
- **Typical Volatility:** High (volatile month-to-month)

### Existing Home Sales
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (~20th of month)
- **Release Time:** 10:00 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** MEDIUM
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Total existing home sales (annualized rate)
  - Median sales price
  - Days on market
  - Months of supply
  - Regional breakdown
- **Significance:** Largest real estate market; consumer wealth effect
- **Typical Volatility:** Moderate-High

### Case-Shiller Home Price Index
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (with 2-month lag)
- **Release Time:** 9:00 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** 2 months prior
- **Market Impact:** MEDIUM
- **Key Metrics:**
  - National index
  - Regional breakdowns (20 metro areas)
  - Month-over-month & year-over-year changes
- **Significance:** Housing market health; wealth effects
- **Typical Volatility:** Low (smoother than other housing data)

### Mortgage Applications (MBA Index)
- **Release Schedule:** Weekly
- **Release Time:** 7:00 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior week
- **Market Impact:** LOW-MEDIUM
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Purchase applications
  - Refi applications
  - Mortgage rates
- **Significance:** Early indicator of housing demand
- **Typical Volatility:** Low

## Fed Policy & Meetings

### FOMC Meeting Decision
- **Release Schedule:** 8 times per year (roughly every 6 weeks)
- **Release Times:**
  - Statement: 2:00 PM ET (Wednesday meetings)
  - Press conference: 2:30 PM ET (every other meeting)
- **Market Impact:** CRITICAL (Highest)
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Fed funds rate target
  - QE/QT decisions
  - Policy guidance
  - Economic projections (quarterly)
  - Dot plot (rate projections)
- **Significance:** Directly sets monetary policy; highest market impact
- **Typical Volatility:** Extreme (200+ point S&P swings possible)

### FOMC Minutes (Delayed Release)
- **Release Schedule:** 3 weeks after FOMC meeting
- **Release Time:** 2:00 PM ET
- **Market Impact:** MEDIUM-HIGH
- **Key Metrics:** Discussion, debate, dissents
- **Significance:** Additional color on Fed thinking
- **Typical Volatility:** Moderate

### Fed Chair & Official Speeches
- **Release Schedule:** Ad-hoc (usually announced in advance)
- **Typical Times:** Various (morning, afternoon, evening)
- **Market Impact:** HIGH (variable)
- **Significance:** Provides policy guidance, market expectations management
- **Typical Volatility:** Variable (depends on content)

## Other Key Indicators

### Durable Goods Orders
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (~25th of month)
- **Release Time:** 8:30 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** MEDIUM
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Total new orders
  - Core durable goods (ex-transportation)
  - Prior month revisions
- **Significance:** Business investment indicator; manufacturing demand
- **Typical Volatility:** High (volatile month-to-month)

### Factory Orders
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (monthly after durable goods)
- **Release Time:** 10:00 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** LOW-MEDIUM
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Total factory orders
  - By industry
- **Significance:** Complements durable goods data
- **Typical Volatility:** Low-Moderate

### Personal Income & Spending
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (end of month)
- **Release Time:** 8:30 AM ET (income), 8:30 AM ET (spending)
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** MEDIUM-HIGH
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Personal income (wages, dividends, benefits)
  - Personal spending/consumption
  - Savings rate
  - Prior month revisions
- **Significance:** Consumer purchasing power and demand
- **Typical Volatility:** Moderate

### Jobless Claims (Longer-term)
- **Continuing Claims:** Weekly (Thursday, 8:30 AM ET)
- **Market Impact:** MEDIUM
- **Significance:** Sustained joblessness; unemployment trend
- **Typical Volatility:** Low-Moderate

### Trade Balance
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (~5 business days after month-end)
- **Release Time:** 8:30 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** MEDIUM
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Goods deficit
  - Services balance
  - Major trading partners
- **Significance:** GDP component; currency implications
- **Typical Volatility:** Moderate

### Business Inventories
- **Release Schedule:** Monthly (mid-month)
- **Release Time:** 10:00 AM ET
- **Data Covers:** Prior month
- **Market Impact:** LOW-MEDIUM
- **Key Metrics:**
  - Total inventories
  - Inventory-to-sales ratio
  - By stage of processing
- **Significance:** Supply-demand imbalances; demand signals
- **Typical Volatility:** Low

- FOMC decisions
- NFP releases

- Retail sales

- Fed minutes
- Non-FOMC Fed speeches
- Durable goods
- Trade balance

- Market positioning ahead of FOMC/CPI/NFP
- Implied volatility expectations
