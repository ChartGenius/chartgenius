## 1️⃣ ONE-PAGER OVERVIEW

### What is TradVue?

TradVue is a **comprehensive trading intelligence platform** that aggregates real-time market data, AI-powered news analysis, and social sentiment into actionable insights for active traders and institutional investors.

### The Problem We Solve

Modern traders face fragmented workflows:
- **Information scattered** across 5+ different platforms (market data, news, sentiment, calendars)
- **Decision paralysis** from information overload and conflicting signals
- **Time waste** switching between apps instead of executing trades
- **FOMO-driven losses** from missing critical news and market events
- **No unified view** of market sentiment vs. actual price movement

### Our Solution

A **single unified platform** that:
- ✅ Delivers **real-time market data** (stocks, forex, crypto, commodities) with 20+ years of history
- ✅ Aggregates **AI-analyzed news** from 100+ sources with sentiment & impact scoring
- ✅ Provides **smart watchlists & alerts** with multi-channel notifications
- ✅ Tracks **social sentiment** and correlates it with price movements
- ✅ Integrates with **economic calendars** and earnings announcements

### Key Differentiators

| Feature | TradVue | Competitors |
|---------|------------|-------------|
| **AI News Analysis** | Automatic sentiment & impact scoring | Manual, delayed insights |
| **Speed** | Built in 2 days, MVP-ready | Months to launch |
| **Tech Stack** | Modern Next.js + Node.js + PostgreSQL | Legacy web tech |
| **AI-First Team** | AI agents + human founder | Traditional teams |
| **Unified Experience** | All-in-one platform | 5-7 fragmented tools |
| **Pricing** | $19/mo Professional tier | $30-100+ per tool |
