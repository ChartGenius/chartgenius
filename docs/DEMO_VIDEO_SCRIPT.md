## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Voiceover
- **Tone:** Confident, fast-paced, but conversational. No corporate-speak.
- **Pace:** ~160-170 words per minute (slightly faster than normal speech—traders are impatient)
- **Voice:** Male or female, clear, with slight urgency (but not panicked)

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep navy or black background
- **Accent:** Teal, electric blue, or bright green (bullish vibes)
- **Text:** White, with green for gains, red for losses
- **UI Elements:** Clean, flat design. No skeuomorphism.

### Music/Sound Design
- **0-10 sec (Hook):** Tense, dramatic strings. Sudden drop for the "missed it" moment.
- **10-30 sec (Solution):** Uplifting, tech-forward synth. Builds energy.
- **30-90 sec (Features):** Energetic but not distracting. Subtle notification pings as features activate.
- **90-100 sec (Social Proof):** Confident, triumphant. Brief pause between stat reveals.
- **100-120 sec (CTA):** Warm, inviting. Soft fade-out.

### Video Format
- **Resolution:** 1080p (1920x1080) or 1440p for YouTube
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
- **Frame Rate:** 60fps (smooth, modern feel)
- **Length:** Exactly 120 seconds (or 118-122 for minor adjustments)

### Animation Style
- **Transitions:** Quick fades and slides (0.2-0.4 sec each)
- **Chart animations:** Data fills in smoothly, not instantly
- **Text:** Appears and disappears cleanly, no distracting wipes
